What is “systemic solvency”?
It is the ability of the financial system as a whole to remain solvent under correlated shocks, contagion and long-horizon structural risks: climate & transition, planetary boundaries, macro-economic stress, and technology-driven vulnerabilities.
Illustrative use cases (non-binding)
  • Systemic Solvency Dashboard for supervisors or research teams.
  • System-wide solvency index by a data / analytics provider.
  • Group-wide “Systemic Solvency Framework” for a global bank or reinsurer.
  • Academic chair or think-tank on systemic risk & financial stability.
  • Scenario platform linking climate, planetary boundaries and macroprudential stress-tests.

Qu’appelle-t-on « solvabilité systémique » ?
C’est la capacité du système financier dans son ensemble à rester solvable face à des chocs corrélés, de la contagion et des risques structurels de long terme : climat & transition, limites planétaires, tensions macro-économiques, vulnérabilités technologiques et d’infrastructures.
